# Code of Conduct

We follow a respectful, inclusive, harassment-free collaboration model.

## Expected Behavior
- Be constructive and kind.
- Assume good intent.
- Give actionable technical feedback.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Doxxing or sharing private data.
- Deliberate sabotage of project discussions.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or ban contributors for violations.
